Who We Are

NSBE B-Section is one of two chapters of the National Society of Black Engineers at Kettering University, organized around the school's alternating academic term schedule. B-Section operates during Kettering's B-term, building a consistent and energized community every time we're on campus.

We are more than a club — we're a professional family. Through technical workshops, career networking events, mentorship programs, and community service, we equip our members with what they need to thrive in engineering and beyond.
